Steps

How Dropshipping Work
Step 01

Set Up Your Online Store

Choose a niche, create your store, and add products from a dropshipping supplier.

Step 02

Customer Places an Order

A customer visits your store and places an order for a product.

Step 03

You Forward the Order to Supplier

You purchase the product from the supplier, who handles packaging and shipping.

Step 04

Supplier Ships Product & You Earn Profit

The supplier ships directly to the customer, and you keep the profit margin.
